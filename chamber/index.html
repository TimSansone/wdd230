Free Thing To Do


Disney Springs

Free admission, covered parking, and dozens of places to shop and eat. Enjoy remarkable architecture, natural beauty, and free family fun that includes live performances and other entertaining interludes.

Address: 1486 Buena Vista Drive, Lake Buena Vista, FL 32830

Hours of operation: 10:00am - 11:00pm

Another Fun Free Thing To Do


West Orange Trail

Enjoy 22.26 miles of paved Trail from the Orange/Lake County line, the town of Killarney and Oakland, the city of Winter Garden, across U.S. 441, through downtown Apopka to Welch Road. Don't forget to stop and enjoy the butterfly garden at the Tildenville outpost!

Address: 501 Crown Point Cross Rd., Winter Garden, FL 34787

Hours: Sunrise to Sunset
