Membership Levels

Non-Profit ($50/month): Access to networking events and business educational programs. Inclusion in the chamber's online directory. Opportunity to participate in community service initiatives. Access to discounted advertising and sponsorships.


Bronze ($100/month): All non-profit membership benefits. Enhanced visibility through a featured listing in the chamber's directory. Opportunity to promote business events through the chamber's communication channels. Access to chamber's resources and market research.


Silver Membership ($200/month): All bronze membership benefits. Exclusive networking opportunities with prominent business leaders. Recognition at chamber events and in promotional materials. Access to professional development workshops and seminars.


Gold Membership ($300/month): All silver membership benefits. Premier visibility and promotion as a top-tier member. Priority access to high-profile chamber events and speaking engagements. Personalized business support and introductions to key stakeholders.
