Tours Offered

Punta Sur Beach Eco Beach Park

Punta Sur marks the southern point of Cozumel and is part of the Parque Punta Sur, an ecological park that covers the reefs, beaches, lagoons, and low forest of the surrounding area. The reef system is also part of the Arrecifes de Cozumel National Park.

Mr. Sanchos Beach Club

One of the best all-around beach spots on Cozumel. It is especially acclaimed for its water sports, including kayaking, snorkeling, sailing and diving. You can rent a dive charter and also grab some grub at the nearby restaurants and bars.

Passion Island

An amazing Cozumel excursion where you get to hold, touch, and even feed real live stingrays! Breathe easy, their barbs have been removed so it's safe, and oh-so-fun! Feel their "Hoover-like" mouths on your hands. It's really exciting!

Cozumel Pearl Farm

San Gervasio is an archaeological site of the pre-Columbian Maya civilization. Located in the northern third of the island of Cozumel off the northeastern coast of the Yucatán Peninsula, in what is now the Mexican state of Quintana Roo.

Chichen Itza

One of the most famous sights and one not to be missed is Punta Molas. A picturesque beach in the northern part of Cozumel. The anchor point here is the old white-and-red lighthouse surrounded by tropical vegetation and secluded beaches.

Tulum Mayan Ruins

Located inside Cozumel´s National Reef Marine Park. Enjoy the beautiful beach and snorkel or scuba dive the most beautiful reef on the island. Plenty of amenities to choose from.
